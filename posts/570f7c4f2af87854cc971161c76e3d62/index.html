linux防火墙查看状态

操作防火墙的命令：

  • 查看防火墙状态：systemctl status firewalld
  • 让防火墙可用：systemctl enable firewalld
  • 让防火墙不可用：systemctl disable firewalld
  • 开启防火墙：systemctl start firewalld
  • 禁用防火墙：systemctl stop firewalld
