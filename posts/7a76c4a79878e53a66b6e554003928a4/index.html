解决：Your idea evaluation has expired. Your session will be limited to 30 minutes. For information on how to upgrade your evaluation software please go to https://www.jetbrains.com/idea/buy/?fromIDE

1、把提示框的x点掉，会自动打开idea。

2、工具栏选择—“Help” -> “Register”，弹窗选择License server方式，地址填入：http://fls.jetbrains-agent.com。

3、点击 Activate 和 Test Connection ，出现提示 “Connection successful”，说明成功了。

4、关掉idea，重新打开即可。
