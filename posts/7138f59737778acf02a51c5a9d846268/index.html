Sub Macro1()
  Dim Rng As Range
    Set Rng = ActiveCell
    Rng.Copy
    ActiveSheet.UsedRange.PasteSpecial Paste:=xlPasteFormats, Operation:=xlNone, _
        SkipBlanks:=False, Transpose:=False
    Application.CutCopyMode = False
    Rng.Select
End Sub

有效！

操作：先用鼠标选择源单元格 --- 再运行上述代码 进行全部格式化
