CentOS安装Metasploit

curl https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rapid7/metasploit-omnibus/master/config/templates/metasploit-framework-wrappers/msfupdate.erb > msfinstall

chmod +x msfinstall

./msfinstall

国内的服务器好像有的没法下载，只能手动访问：https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rapid7/metasploit-omnibus/master/config/templates/metasploit-framework-wrappers/msfupdate.erb到本地，命名成msfinstall

然后执行后面的两句即可
