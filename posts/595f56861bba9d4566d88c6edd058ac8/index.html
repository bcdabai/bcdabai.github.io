add_subdirectory given source
"/opt/llvm-14.0.3.src/../third-party/benchmark" which is not an existing
  directory.

https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/issues/54941

vi CMakeCache.txt
LLVM_INCLUDE_BENCHMARKS:BOOL=OFF
