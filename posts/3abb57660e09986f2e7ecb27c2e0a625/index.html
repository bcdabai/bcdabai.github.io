TeX中如何对bib文件中的条目注释

对一些项进行注释

如bib文件中参考文献条目中的一些项注释，可以通过改变项名来实现，因为tex对于编译时会忽略无意义的项。例如，将下面的title和year注释：

@book{Gelman03,
	Author = {Andrew Gelman and John B. Carlin and Hal S. Stern and Donald B. Rubin},
	Isbn = {158488388X},
	Publisher = {Chapman and Hall/CRC},
	Title = {Bayesian Data Analysis},
	Year = {2003}}

注释后的形式为：

@book{Gelman03,
	Author = {Andrew Gelman and John B. Carlin and Hal S. Stern and Donald B. Rubin},
	Isbn = {158488388X},
	Publisher = {Chapman and Hall/CRC},
	XTitle = {Bayesian Data Analysis},
	XYear = {2003}}

因为XTitle和XYear是无意义的项。

对整个文献注释

bib文件中对某一个参考文献条目注释，可以通过去掉前面的@实现。例如：

@book{Gelman03,
	Author = {Andrew Gelman and John B. Carlin and Hal S. Stern and Donald B. Rubin},
	Isbn = {158488388X},
	Publisher = {Chapman and Hall/CRC},
	Title = {Bayesian Data Analysis},
	Year = {2003}}

注释后的结果是：

book{Gelman03,
	Author = {Andrew Gelman and John B. Carlin and Hal S. Stern and Donald B. Rubin},
	Isbn = {158488388X},
	Publisher = {Chapman and Hall/CRC},
	Title = {Bayesian Data Analysis},
	Year = {2003}}
