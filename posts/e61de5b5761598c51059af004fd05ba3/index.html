localStorage创建、清除、读取

1.创建缓存

 localStorage.setItem('userInfo', userInfo);

2.读取缓存

  localStorage.getItem('doEdit', this.doEdit);

3.清除所有缓存

localStorage.clear();

4 清楚指定缓存

localStorage.removeItem('userinfo');
