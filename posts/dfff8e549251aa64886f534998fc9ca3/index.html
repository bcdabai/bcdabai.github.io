HTML标题标签（h1~6）用法

 很简单，直接上代码：

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
    <meta charset="UTF-8">
    <meta http-equiv="X-UA-Compatible" content="IE=edge">
    <meta name="viewport" content="width=<device-width>, initial-scale=1.0">
    <title>Document</title>
</head>
<body>
    <h1>这是一级标题</h1>
    <h2>这是二级标题</h2>
    <h3>这是三级标题</h3>
    <h4>这是4级标题</h4>
    <h5>这是5级标题</h5>
    <h6>这是6级标题</h6>
</body>
</html>
